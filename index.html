Cal High Investment Club

What is the Cal High Investment Club?

The Cal High Investment Club aims to foster financial literacy through educating students about investing in stocks, stock options, and other ways of growing money. We believe investing is not something exclusive to “experts” and that teens should take an active role in investing and learn how to invest to achieve their financial goals.
